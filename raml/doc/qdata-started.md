### 约定

* **GET**请求

    > `get`请求的参数都在url中传递

    example:
        http://127.0.0.1:11100/qdata/clusters?cluster_id=1

* **POST、PUT、DELETE**请求

    > `post`、`put`、`delete`请求参数都在`body`中传递

    example:
        {
            "cluster_id": 1
        }
